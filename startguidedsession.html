Start Your Guided Session

Welcome to your first guided workout session with GuideFit!
Follow the steps below to ensure a safe and effective workout experience:

  1. Prepare Your Space: Find a safe, open area free of
    obstacles to perform your exercises.
  2. Warm-Up: Begin with the recommended warm-up
    routine to prepare your body for exercise.
  3. Follow the Guidance: Listen to audio cues and watch
    visual prompts to maintain proper form throughout your workout.
  4. Stay Hydrated: Keep water nearby and take breaks
    as needed to stay hydrated.
  5. Cool Down: After completing your workout, follow
    the cooldown routine to help your body recover.
  6. Provide Feedback: After your session, consider
    providing feedback to help us improve your experience.
